Our Story

Where craftsmanship meets timeless elegance.

How It All Began

CBVS was born from a simple idea: create jewelry that feels personal, meaningful, and truly lasting. What started as a small studio crafting handmade pieces evolved into a brand trusted for its dedication to refined detail and timeless design.

Every piece is made with intention — blending traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics. We design for those who appreciate quiet luxury, thoughtful artistry, and jewelry that becomes part of their everyday story.

Our Mission

We believe jewelry should do more than shine — it should tell a story. Our mission is to create pieces that carry emotion, celebrate individuality, and inspire connection.

From ethical materials to meticulous finishing, every detail reflects our commitment to quality and authenticity. When you wear CBVS, you wear artistry shaped with passion.
