FAQs

Do you offer a lifetime warranty?
Yes — all pieces come with our lifetime warranty covering manufacturing defects. Wear-and-tear and accidental damage are handled via our repair service (fees may apply).
How long does shipping take?
Standard international shipping typically takes 7–14 business days. We offer expedited options at checkout for faster delivery.
Can I return or exchange an item?
Yes — returns and exchanges are accepted within 30 days of delivery for unworn items in original condition. See our Returns page for full details.
Do you use real gemstones and precious metals?
Always. We source genuine metals and ethically-sourced gemstones. Product pages list material specifications for each item.
How do I care for my jewelry?
Store pieces separately, avoid harsh chemicals, and gently clean with a soft cloth. For deep cleaning or repairs, contact our support team.
Can I request custom or engraved pieces?
Yes — we offer custom work and engraving. Contact us with your idea and we'll provide options and a quote.
